App Registry

Atlassian Cloud · Representative sample · ~35–40 apps evaluated across all three products
What this registry covers
This registry documents installed Marketplace apps across Jira Software, Confluence, and Jira Service Management. It includes apps that are currently active as well as apps that were removed during yearly audits. Apps that were evaluated and declined were not installed and are not tracked here.
Why this exists
Without a catalog, it's impossible to audit the instance or answer basic questions: what does this app do, which product is it tied to, where is the documentation, and is it still being used? The registry answers all of those without anyone having to dig through the Marketplace admin panel or ask around.

About this sample: The active instance included approximately 35–40 apps across all three Atlassian products. This registry shows a representative sample. App names and some details have been generalized to protect confidential information. Tempo Timesheets and ScriptRunner for Jira are documented with their real names and resources. Both were specifically called out in the evaluation process as significant installations.

Active = currently installed and in use
Removed = uninstalled during a yearly audit
