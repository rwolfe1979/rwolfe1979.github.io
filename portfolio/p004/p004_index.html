The Problem

A department needed to move their request handling into JSM. They had a process that worked in email and spreadsheets, and they needed it to survive the migration without losing anything they depended on.

That meant understanding their current process before touching any configuration. Requirements first, build second. The service desk had to fit how they actually worked, not how I expected them to work.
