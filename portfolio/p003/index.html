The Problem

Leadership wanted visibility into project health across departments. The ask was vague: "a report." Before building anything, I spent time figuring out what questions they actually needed to answer and what decisions would follow from the data.

Once the real requirements were clear, I evaluated three options: native Jira dashboards, a Marketplace app, and an Excel-based approach. The Excel solution won on cost, flexibility, and maintainability. The person who requested it can run it without calling me.
