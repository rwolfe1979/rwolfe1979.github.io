Automation Rule Registry

Administrator-owned rules only  ·  Last reviewed: January 2026
Download PDF ↗
Scope
This registry covers administrator-owned rules only, rules built and maintained at the global or multi-project level. Project admins can create their own rules within their projects. Those are not tracked here, but a template is available for project admins who want consistent documentation.
Why This Exists
Undocumented automation rules create maintenance problems. Rules fire unexpectedly. Nobody knows what a rule was supposed to do. Changes break things. This registry answers three questions for anyone maintaining the environment: what does this do, why does it exist, and who owns the business logic.

Change requests: To create, modify, or disable an admin-owned rule, submit a ticket through the Jira Request Portal. Changes are not made in response to verbal or chat requests. Rules that are no longer needed are disabled, not deleted, and the registry is updated with the disablement date and reason.

Active = currently running
Disabled = turned off but preserved for reference
