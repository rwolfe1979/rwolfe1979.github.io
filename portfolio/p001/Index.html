P-001 · Intake Service Desk

Intake Service Desk with Auto-Routing

IT requests were going into a single inbox with no routing, no SLA, and no way to tell who was supposed to handle what. I sat with stakeholders from four departments, documented the current mess, and built a JSM intake portal that auto-categorized requests and routed linked child tickets to the right department project. Manual triage dropped to zero.

The Problem

IT requests came in through email with no structure. No categories, no priority, no routing. Whoever checked the inbox first decided what got worked. Some requests sat for days because the right person never saw them. Others got lost entirely.

Four departments were involved in IT request handling and none of them had visibility into what the others were doing. There was no SLA, no queue, and no way to report on volume or resolution time.
